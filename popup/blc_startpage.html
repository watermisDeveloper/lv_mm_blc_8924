To create a balance, the estimated water resources are compared to different water demand scenarios. These scenarios depend on a year and normally three different demand estimations. High 2040 e.g. are the estimations for high water demand (high population growth, increasing industrial growth, climate change etc.) in the year 2040.

The resources sets are given in quantiles. RsQ05 therefore means the 5%-Qunatile. This ist he amount of water with a non-exceedence probalility of 5%. In easy words this means: 

„with a probability of 5% the resources of the given catchment are smaller than the given set RsQ05“
